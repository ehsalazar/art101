Lab 14: Debugging Tools & Strategies

The focus of this lab was to use debugging knowledge to fix old assignments.

Challenge

The goal of this lab was to take what we’ve learned over the last few weeks and apply it to one of our old labs. Particularly, if we had a lab that we struggled with or one that wasn’t working the way we expected. This gave us the opportunity to debug the issues and hopefully fix them.

Problems

I didn’t have any major issues with past labs. For the most part, I have been able to get the labs completed as expected. That said, there are definitely some opportunities. I decided to give Lab 11 another go.

Debugging

Previously, if a button in one of the sections was clicked, it changed the styling of that section. This occurred regardless if another section already had the special styling applied. My second attempt of the lab now allows only one section to have the special styling at a time. When a section button is clicked, it now applies the special styling to that section, while also removing the special styling from the others.

Results

At the end of the day, this wasn’t a major rewrite, and again, Lab 11 was already working. However, I like the new functionality and am happy with what the debugging has allowed.

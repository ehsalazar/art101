Lab 4: Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

The focus of this lab was to get in the habit of thinking in terms of pseudocoding before jumping into coding.

Challenges

Coming up with a pseudocode list for an everyday task and a computer task were straight forward. The challenge was converting the computer task into a list with programming in mind.

Problems

No problems this go around.

Results

Working as a pair, Selena and I were able to successfully come up with our three pseudocode lists.

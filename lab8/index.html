Lab 8: Anon Functions and Callbacks

The focus of this lab was to experiment with anonymous functions and callbacks.

Challenges

This lab was fun. Getting everything to log to the console worked with little issue. Getting everything into my HTML was more challenging. I suspected that I could use something like onload to have the scripts output into the page rather than the console. Upon investigating, I found that defer might be a better choice, and was the one I went with.

Problems

Most of the problems I encountered came courtesy of the bonus tasks. Once I was able to get my output into my HTML with document.getElementById(); and defer, I had to iterate with innerHTML. I initially set it equal to a constructed string. After testing, I realized I needed to use += to maintain all of the previous test statements.

Results

The lab was challenging, but as mentioned, enjoyable. In the end, I was able to get my the functions to log both to the console and to my webpage. Check it out.
